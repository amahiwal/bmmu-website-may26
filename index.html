The Core Focus

The BMMU Big 5

The landscape has changed. These are the five pillars shaping the programme this year.

01

BE NOTICED

Make your work land in a world that’s hard to break through. It’s about being more relevant, more interesting, and more worth noticing.

02

BE CHOSEN

Build a brand people choose. Focus on what makes brands stand out, stick in people’s minds, and earn preference over time.

03

BE VALUED

Prove the impact of your work where it matters most. Connect your work to what the business actually cares about.

04

BE RIGHT

Know what works — and make better decisions because of it. Turn insight into better decisions — not just more reporting.

05

BE HUMAN

The skills that shape how marketers think, listen and show up. Understanding perception and the impact marketing has on people.

Conclusion

At the end of the day

When you strip it all back, what really matters? After a day of ideas, perspectives and challenges, this is where it all comes together.

A final moment to step back, reflect, and focus on what’s worth taking forward — and what it means for how you approach your work from here.
