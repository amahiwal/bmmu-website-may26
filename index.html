BMMU 2026

BMMU is designed to help marketers think clearly about their work and leave with practical ideas they can actually use.

It is a one-day marketing event taking place on 6th November 2026, created for marketers who want thoughtful, useful clarity away from the noise.

BMMU looks at the job of marketing from multiple angles: brand, creative, PR and the commercial realities of the role, without hype, fluff or sales pitches.
